ABOUT US

The Center Of One Welfare-Africa (COOW-Africa) is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving health and welfare for communities across Africa through sustainable and community-centered approaches.

Our mission is to ensure access to quality healthcare and improve welfare for all communities across Africa, with a special focus on vulnerable populations in rural and underserved areas.

Founded in (year), COOW-Africa is committed to expanding its operations across the continent, working closely with local partners, governments, and international organizations to create lasting impact.

RESEARCH

At COOW-Africa, research is at the core of our evidence-based approach. We conduct and support research in areas related to public health, healthcare delivery, and social determinants of health in African contexts.

Our programs focus on generating evidence to inform policy and practice. We collaborate with academic institutions, research centers, and international organizations to enhance our capacity and impact.
